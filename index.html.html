About :


TechiePedia is the project started by the group inner circle to disseminate information about some well known, estabilished and popular individuals who are well known in the world of computer software and technology.
Techiepedia focuses on the entire lives of these persons starting from their journeys from the lowermost levels to pinnacle of success . It outlines their sacrifices,dedication and their intrests which lead to their success. It also accounts for the role of the other peaople who played a major role for their success.
TechiePedia will allways try to live upto its aim to inspire the future generation of technocrats. Thus its always requested from its readers to never to shy away from giving any suggestions and feel free to contact the team.
